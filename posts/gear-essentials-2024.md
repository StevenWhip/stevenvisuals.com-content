# My Minimalist Gear List

Traveling light has changed my photography. It allows me to walk further, climb higher, and be less conspicuous in street settings.

## The Body

I've switched entirely to mirrorless. The weight savings are undeniable.

## The Lenses

I strictly carry two primes:
* **35mm f/1.4**: For environmental portraits and street.
* **85mm f/1.8**: For tight portraits and details.

## Accessories

* Extra batteries (always)
* A lightweight carbon fiber tripod
* ND filters for long exposures
