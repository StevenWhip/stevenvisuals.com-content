# Seeing in Monochrome

Black and white photography is more than just a filter you apply in post-processing. It's a way of seeing the world.

When color is removed, you are left with the raw building blocks of an image:

1. **Light and Shadow**
2. **Texture**
3. **Shape and Form**

## Emotion in Contrast

High contrast images can convey drama and power, while low contrast images often feel ethereal and dreamlike.
